About

Hi, I’m Erick Morell — a multidisciplinary artist, designer, and fabricator with a Bachelor’s Degree in Entertainment Design from the Art Institute of Pittsburgh (Class of 2017).

My work spans traditional painting and sculpture, murals, theatrical props and makeup, concept art, and 3D design. I combine creativity with precision to bring ideas to life from concept to completion.

For over a decade, I’ve collaborated with established companies while maintaining an independent freelance practice and developing personal passion projects. I strive to blend artistic vision with technical expertise to create distinctive, compelling work that leaves a lasting impression.
